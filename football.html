Football


Football, also known as soccer in some countries is a sport played between two teams which consists of 11 players in each side. Football is played with a spherical ball. Each Team consists of 10 actively moving players and a goal keeper to stop the opposition from scoring a goal. Every player is allowed to manure the ball with any body part except from the hand. Only the goalkeeper is allowed to use the hand to catch the ball and that is also allowed in the penalty area

Main objective of a team is to outscore it's opposition and win the game. Goal is scored when a team scores it past the goal line between the goal posts. Football kit is a T-shirt, a short and socks. Along with that players are required or must wear protective gear depending on pitch conditions. Boots, studs, blades, shin pads and ankle pads are the protective gears used by Football players. Goalkeepers are allowed to use wear gloves

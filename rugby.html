Rugby


Rugby, is a full contact team sport which was originated in early 19 th around England. Rugby is a popular sport around the globe played men as well as women. The game of Rugby was spreaded across the Great Briatain and Ireland which was sooner accompanied by the countries in British Empire. These countries include Australia, New Zealand, South Africa and France. Even the countries Fiji,Georgia,Madagascar,New Zealand Samoa and Tonga has declared Rugby as their National Sport.

For Rugby there are 2 main formats in the game. First is 15-a-side fixture in which two teams consisted of 15 of men or women per each team is competing for a total of 80 mins (two 40 mins halves). The other form of the game will be 7-a-side fixture with 7 men in a team and the game time last for a shorter period of 14 mins.Rugby officialy made it's way to the Olympics in 2016. But even before that Rugby had the precesnce in the world Sports Calender by having The Rugby World Cup every four years. The most recent winners in 2019 was South Africa SpringBoks.

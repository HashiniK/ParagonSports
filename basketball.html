Basketball


Invented in 1891 by Canadian - American gym teacher James Naismith in Sprigerfield, Massachusetts,United States, basketball has evoled to become one of the world's most famous and widely viewed sport. Basketball made it's way to Olympic in 1904.

Basketball is played by a team of 5 men or women. Basketball is known to be the second fastest sport played in world. Therefore the players playing basketball must have a physical strength as well as agility.

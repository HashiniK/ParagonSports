Cricket


Among hundreds of sports which are in all around the world , Cricket takes a special place because of thousands of reasons and uncountable values. It’s been around four centuries since cricket started as a sport. In very early periods this unbelievable sport was in the minds of men as a pride of Britain’s . Because of that people were too afraid of playing this game without the permissions of Britain’s. If we pay the attention towards the history , it tells cricket was started in South-East England . And very soon it spread globally with the power of Britain under the British empire. That’s how this came to other countries.

Cricket is well known as a team game. Two teams with eleven players of each , play for victory by using a bat and a ball can clearly seen in this game. One team must take enough points more than the other team if they want the Victory .Because of that maybe in early days it’s called as “ a bat and ball game “ . As bowling and fielding, Cricket separates into two parts. Each team must act these two parts. And there’re some rules in this game , help for a better battle and for a fair judgment. And these rules are held in a code known as lows of cricket which is maintained by Melbourne Cricket Club ( MCC ) .

In this current century not only men but women slo play cricket. It can be told as a great victory of these human beings. And it has nominated as a summer Olympic game also. In this long journey, cricket took a very precious place in the whole world.

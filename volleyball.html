Volleyball


As we know Sri Lanka is a very small country located in South Asia , under India . This little paradise has got an amazing sport as its national game Called as volleyball. This has taken a special part in sports nowadays. History gives a clear understanding about how volleyball begun. In 1895 sir William G. Morgen found Volleyball as a combination of Baseball, Basketball and Tennis. In early days it was Called as “mintonette” and by time passed it called as volleyball. After that it spread into two types as indoor and outdoor. beach and grass can put into the type of outdoor . Very first in United States in 1895 had a match of volleyball and after that lot of countries gave a special place for this in their own countries and in their sports committees.

There are two teams with six members in each play this with a large ball in a court which is separated into two parts by a net. This game can be played as both indoor and outdoor game as mentioned above before. And it can be taken as an athletic sport also . There are some differences which help to separate whether it’s an outdoor one or an indoor one. Balls use in indoor playings are much heavier than outdoor balls and made by leather also. Beach volleyballs , which is also known as outdoor volleyballs are completely opposite to indoor balls.

Victory is completely defend on the scores taken by according to the rules and regulations by both teams. If the left side team’s ball touches the right side team’s ground it means the left team got a point. This is how volleyball normally plays. Except this there are some another rules also. Whatever volleyball can name as a energetic and mind relaxing sport which is extremely suitable for both men and women.
